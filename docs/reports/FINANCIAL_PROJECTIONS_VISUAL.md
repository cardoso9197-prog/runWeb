## Driver Earnings Analysis

```
Why 30 Drivers for 3 Months Works:

Average Driver Daily Earnings:
- 15 rides/day Ã— 2,000 XOF fare = 30,000 XOF/day
- Driver keeps 80% = 24,000 XOF/day ($40/day)
- 30 days/month = 720,000 XOF/month ($1,200/month)

This is EXCELLENT earnings in Guinea-Bissau where:
- Average monthly income: $150-250
- Drivers earn 4-6x average income
- High driver satisfaction = low churn
- Word-of-mouth recruitment easier

If we started with 100 drivers immediately:
- Only 4-5 rides/day per driver
- 10,000 XOF/day ($16/day)
- 240,000 XOF/month ($400/month)
- Lower than market average
- High driver churn
- Poor service quality
```
